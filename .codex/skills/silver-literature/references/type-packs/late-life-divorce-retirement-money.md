# Type Pack: Late-Life Divorce / Retirement Money

Use for 熟年離婚, 定年離婚, long marriage betrayal, retirement bonus, pension, severance, affair, cold spouse, and second start after divorce.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as structural references: divorce paper timing, retirement money trap, affair proof, financial boundary, and calm exit. If available, read `对标/late-life-divorce-retirement-money/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The story begins when long marriage is suddenly reduced to money, convenience, or contempt.
- Retirement money, pension, house, savings, or insurance creates the trap.
- The protagonist's calm works because they prepared legally or emotionally.
- The reversal clarifies who depended on whom.
- Recovery should not be only "the spouse regrets it"; the protagonist must gain a concrete next life.

## Six-Episode Season

1. Divorce bomb: papers appear at retirement, hospital, anniversary, or family meal.
2. Money trail: pension, severance, savings, or affair spending becomes visible.
3. False compliance: protagonist signs nothing or signs strategically after preparing proof.
4. Legal reveal: lawyer, bank, company, or registry shows the real boundary.
5. Dependence collapse: spouse/lover/child loses the hidden subsidy.
6. Second start: apartment key, job, travel, old friend, or peaceful breakfast.

## Required Payoffs

- Divorce/retirement trigger.
- Financial proof.
- Legal or bank venue.
- Calm protagonist choice.
- Spouse dependence exposed.
- Concrete second start.

## Avoid

- Cheating revenge without late-life stakes.
- Secret wealth with no setup.
- Ending only on the antagonist crying.
