# Type Pack: Workplace Retirement / Reputation

Use for retirement party insult, demotion, ageism, company founder, old clients, employee ID, shareholder list, technical skill, and professional reputation restored.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as structural references: ageist workplace insult, old merit, client/founder proof, public company venue, and successor recognition. If available, read `对标/workplace-retirement-reputation/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The protagonist is treated as obsolete after decades of work.
- Younger staff, family company, boss, or client-facing venue creates public humiliation.
- Proof must tie to real contribution: employee ID, old contract, client list, patent/design, shareholder register, founding photo, license, craft tool.
- Reversal shows that continuity, trust, and memory are assets.
- Recovery may be mentorship, consulting, own shop, proper retirement, or public thanks.

## Six-Episode Season

1. Workplace insult: retirement party, demotion, reception, or client meeting humiliation.
2. Old contribution: object/record hints at protagonist's true role.
3. False exit: protagonist appears to leave quietly.
4. Client/proof turn: customer, board member, register, or contract reveals value.
5. Reputation rebound: company loses or restores trust based on the protagonist.
6. Chosen work: protagonist mentors, consults, retires, or starts a small craft life.

## Required Payoffs

- Ageism/public insult.
- Professional proof.
- Client/company witness.
- Reputation consequence.
- Work identity restored.

## Avoid

- Generic corporate revenge without senior-life stakes.
- Overpowered CEO twist with no history.
- Treating retirement as failure.
