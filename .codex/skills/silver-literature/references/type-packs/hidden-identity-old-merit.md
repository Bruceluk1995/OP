# Type Pack: Hidden Identity / Old Merit

Use for underestimated retired expert, former founder, hidden owner, professional license, craft skill, old client network, donor record, and public recognition after humiliation.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as structural references: public underestimation, old credential, witness recognition, and merit restored. If available, read `对标/hidden-identity-old-merit/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The protagonist is dismissed as "just" a cleaner, housewife, retired worker, old parent, guard, or poor spouse.
- Their real value must be seeded: skill habit, old photo, name on document, client memory, license, tool, company register, shareholder list.
- The reveal should prove long contribution, not random status.
- The antagonist's arrogance collapses because they attacked the foundation they relied on.
- Recovery can be work return, mentorship, quiet pride, or chosen retirement.

## Six-Episode Season

1. Public insult: protagonist is humiliated in family/company/community.
2. Merit seed: a small habit/object hints at old expertise.
3. False low status: antagonist overreaches because they think status is gone.
4. Witness arrives: client, board member, doctor, banker, or elder recognizes them.
5. Status correction: register/license/old contract proves the truth.
6. Dignity choice: protagonist returns, retires on their terms, or mentors someone.

## Required Payoffs

- Underestimation.
- Seeded old merit.
- Public venue.
- Witness or document.
- Recognition tied to contribution.
- Dignified choice.

## Avoid

- Sudden billionaire twist with no setup.
- Status reveal that erases emotional stakes.
- Making age only disguise.
