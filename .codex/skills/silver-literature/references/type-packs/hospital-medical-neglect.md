# Type Pack: Hospital / Medical Neglect

Use for hospital calls, diagnosis, consent forms, medical neglect, stolen surgery money, isolation, injury evidence, nurse/doctor witness, and care safety intervention.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as structural references: medical warning, professional witness, document proof, safety intervention, and restrained emotional stakes. If available, read `对标/hospital-medical-neglect/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The protagonist or someone they protect is medically vulnerable.
- Family neglect, money theft, false explanation, or refusal of treatment creates the pressure.
- Proof must be concrete: diagnosis, consent form, emergency call log, nurse note, medicine record, injury pattern, payment receipt.
- A professional witness changes "family matter" into public accountability.
- Recovery prioritizes safety, care arrangement, health, and dignity.

## Six-Episode Season

1. Medical bomb: hospital call, diagnosis, missing money, or ignored symptom.
2. Pattern evidence: medicine, appointment, consent, or injury record contradicts the family story.
3. False compliance: protagonist waits, transfers records, or calls a professional.
4. Witness turn: doctor, nurse, lawyer, police, or facility worker speaks.
5. Safety consequence: treatment, police visit, care transfer, or financial freeze.
6. Recovery: clinic visit, safe room, proper care, or calm meal.

## Required Payoffs

- Medical stake.
- Professional record.
- Witness with authority.
- Safety intervention.
- Protagonist action.
- Gentle recovery.

## Avoid

- Gore or voyeuristic suffering.
- Medical miracles without process.
- Using illness only to make revenge louder.
