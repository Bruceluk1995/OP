# Type Pack: Property / Inheritance / Rights

Use for 遺産相続, house deed, property title, family registry, bankbook, will, stolen assets, in-laws treating the protagonist's home as theirs, and legal boundary reversal.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as structural references: ownership pressure, document proof, family greed, legal venue, and home-as-dignity payoff. If available, read `对标/property-inheritance-rights/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The family treats the protagonist's money, house, land, or inheritance as shared prey.
- The protagonist knows or discovers a legal boundary others ignored.
- Proof must be procedural: deed, will, registry, bankbook, tax record, lawyer letter, inheritance division, insurance, or transfer record.
- Public reversal happens in lawyer office, bank, family meeting, real-estate office, funeral, or registry office.
- The aftertaste should restore "home" as safety, not only assets.

## Six-Episode Season

1. Property grab: someone demands a house, room, account, or inheritance.
2. Old right: title, will, or registry appears as ordinary paper.
3. False compliance: protagonist lets greed expose itself.
4. Legal proof: document is read in a place where it matters.
5. Access cut: card, key, sale, inheritance, or move changes power.
6. Home restored: protagonist chooses who may enter their life.

## Required Payoffs

- Asset pressure.
- Legal document.
- Greed overreach.
- Official venue.
- Ownership consequence.
- Home boundary.

## Avoid

- Inheritance tricks with no emotional debt.
- Legal reversal without documents.
- Treating the protagonist as only rich, not dignified.
