# Type Pack: Adult Child / Filial Reckoning

Use for adult children exploiting parents, son/daughter-in-law demands, abandoned parent, grandchild witness, remittance, nursing burden, and parent-child reconciliation or rupture.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as structural references: adult-child entitlement, money/care records, grandchild witness, family meeting, and boundary setting. If available, read `对标/adult-child-filial-reckoning/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The child treats the parent as wallet, caregiver, housekeeper, or backup housing.
- The protagonist has supported the family longer than anyone admits.
- Proof is everyday and financial: bank transfers, childcare schedule, house key, school fee, care record, phone log, grandchild line.
- The reversal makes the adult child face the support they consumed.
- Ending can be reconciliation, boundary, or clean separation.

## Six-Episode Season

1. Child's demand: money, care, housing, apology, or obedience is demanded.
2. Support ledger: old transfers, childcare, or house labor appears.
3. False compliance: parent appears to continue helping while preparing boundary.
4. Witness turn: grandchild, spouse, banker, neighbor, or lawyer reveals facts.
5. Reckoning: support stops, debt is named, or family role is rewritten.
6. Parent's life: protagonist chooses a smaller but freer family shape.

## Required Payoffs

- Adult-child entitlement.
- Support proof.
- Witness or family venue.
- Boundary-setting line.
- Relationship consequence.
- Recovery beyond parent role.

## Avoid

- Demonizing all children without nuance.
- Reconciliation without accountability.
- Making the parent passive until rescued.
