# Type Pack: Pension / Bank / Scam

Use for 年金, bank passbook, pension theft, frozen card, scam calls, family account control, fraud, police/banker witness, and retirement money safety.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as structural references: account access, scam pressure, bank counter reveal, police report, and money-boundary recovery. If available, read `对标/pension-fraud-bank-scam/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The protagonist's old-age money is treated as family property or scam target.
- Pressure comes from child, spouse, in-law, neighbor, romance scammer, caregiver, or fake professional.
- Proof is procedural: passbook, ATM footage, transfer record, pension notice, voice recording, bank freeze, police report.
- The public venue is bank, police, family meeting, lawyer office, or municipal counter.
- Recovery shows ordinary financial autonomy: breakfast, medicine paid, rent safe, small hobby.

## Six-Episode Season

1. Money shock: card fails, pension is missing, or someone demands access.
2. Account trail: passbook, notice, transfer, or call log reveals pattern.
3. False compliance: protagonist lets scammer/family member make one more move.
4. Bank witness: teller, police, lawyer, or municipal worker confirms fraud.
5. Freeze and consequence: access is cut, debt named, report filed, or family exposed.
6. Autonomy restored: protagonist buys food, medicine, ticket, or small comfort with their own money.

## Required Payoffs

- Pension/account pressure.
- Transaction proof.
- Bank/police/professional witness.
- Access cut or report.
- Financial autonomy image.

## Avoid

- Unrealistic instant money recovery.
- Scam details that teach fraud tactics.
- Ending only with cash, not dignity.
