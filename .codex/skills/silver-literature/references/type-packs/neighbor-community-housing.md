# Type Pack: Neighbor / Community / Housing

Use for neighborhood association, old house, housing boundary, parking/road disputes, local gossip, community witness, old shop, and public apology in a small social world.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as structural references: local contempt, old house key, security footage, neighborhood witness, and community reversal. If available, read `对标/neighbor-community-housing/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The protagonist is misread in a small community: poor old person, nuisance, lonely widow, useless tenant, or weak homeowner.
- Pressure comes through neighbors, relatives, landlords, redevelopment, parking, shared road, trash, local association, or old-shop gossip.
- Proof is local and physical: key, deed, security footage, neighborhood minutes, old photo, shop ledger, municipal notice, repair invoice.
- The reversal is social: apology, restored access, legal boundary, community trust, or public shame.
- Recovery should keep the protagonist rooted or let them leave by choice.

## Six-Episode Season

1. Local insult: neighbor/family/community humiliates protagonist over house or status.
2. Old tie: key, deed, photo, ledger, or local memory hints at the truth.
3. Pressure escalates: eviction threat, rumor, blocked road, or redevelopment.
4. Witness/proof: camera, minutes, city office, old neighbor, or lawyer confirms.
5. Community reversal: apology, boundary, payment, access, or reputation changes.
6. Home light: protagonist keeps, sells, repairs, or leaves the house by choice.

## Required Payoffs

- Local social pressure.
- Housing/community proof.
- Public witness.
- Boundary correction.
- Home or departure choice.

## Avoid

- Making neighbors cartoonishly cruel without social logic.
- Housing dispute without documents.
- Ending with shame only, no restored place.
