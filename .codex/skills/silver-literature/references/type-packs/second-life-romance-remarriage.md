# Type Pack: Late-Life Romance / Remarriage

Use for 老いらくの恋, late-life romance, widow/widower companionship, remarriage, gentle second marriage, family opposition, and healing after divorce or bereavement.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as structural references: companionship, family opposition, practical tenderness, old wound, and public choosing. If available, read `对标/second-life-romance-remarriage/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- Romance is not youthful fantasy; it is companionship after loss, divorce, care, or loneliness.
- The couple proves love through practical care, respect for boundaries, health routines, hobbies, meals, and family negotiations.
- Family opposition often comes from inheritance anxiety, shame, control, or fear of replacement.
- Proof may be letters, medical appointments, shared receipts, old promise, witness, or recording of family contempt.
- Ending should show dignity, consent, and ordinary future.

## Six-Episode Season

1. Encounter or proposal: late-life affection becomes visible under family pressure.
2. Old wound: divorce, death, care, or loneliness explains the hesitation.
3. Family opposition: child/in-law/neighbor shames the relationship.
4. Practical proof: the partner protects dignity through action, not possession.
5. Public choosing: family meeting, hospital, facility, or ceremony tests the bond.
6. Quiet future: shared breakfast, key, garden, travel, hobby, or second wedding.

## Required Payoffs

- Mature companionship.
- Family/social opposition.
- Practical tenderness.
- Consent and boundary.
- Public choosing.
- Ordinary future image.

## Avoid

- Possessive romance.
- Ignoring inheritance/care consequences.
- Making romance erase the protagonist's independence.
