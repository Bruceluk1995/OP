# Type Pack: Nursing Home / Facility / Elder Housing

Use for 老人ホーム, care facility, dementia, elder housing, facility bills, resident witness, care plan, family abandonment, and safe-place decisions.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as structural references: facility pressure, resident memory object, care plan proof, housing safety, and autonomy. If available, read `对标/nursing-home-facility/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The protagonist's housing or care safety is at stake.
- Family may abandon, over-control, dump responsibility, steal facility money, or dismiss dementia/decline.
- Proof comes from care plan, facility bill, visitor log, staff note, medication record, resident testimony, or preserved memory object.
- The reversal should protect autonomy and safety, not exploit dementia for shock.
- Recovery means safe housing, chosen care, resident dignity, or family boundary.

## Six-Episode Season

1. Facility wound: protagonist is placed, threatened, neglected, or lied about.
2. Care reality: bill, note, staff/resident witness reveals hidden pressure.
3. False compliance: protagonist or ally quietly records/checks facts.
4. Safety proof: facility, doctor, lawyer, or social worker confirms abuse/fraud.
5. Housing decision: care plan, police, transfer, or contract changes power.
6. Safe place: protagonist gains a room, routine, friend, hobby, or chosen visitor.

## Required Payoffs

- Housing/care safety stake.
- Facility record.
- Staff/resident/professional witness.
- Autonomy choice.
- Family/facility consequence.
- Safe-place image.

## Avoid

- Using dementia as a gimmick.
- Facility horror without proof logic.
- Ignoring the protagonist's agency.
