# Type Pack: Palace / Family Conflict / Inheritance

Use for 王宮, palace intrigue, family coldness, inheritance, disinheritance, divorce, remarriage, palace office work, and domestic political pressure. Do not use this pack for childcare, step-parenting, contract-parent, custody, adoption, guardianship, orphan rescue, or family reconstruction.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as structural references: family ledger, inheritance rule, palace office competence, marriage/family pressure, romantic alliance, and public legal reversal. If available, read `对标/palace-family-inheritance/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The wound is domestic and social: family neglect, stolen inheritance, illegitimate accusation, divorce, remarriage stigma, marital property conflict, or palace labor exploitation.
- The proof object is often a will, ledger, birth record, tax file, marriage contract, household account, office memo, or witness.
- The heroine's agency is practical: administration, negotiation, etiquette, household reform, bookkeeping, legal memory, or emotional steadiness.
- Romance is lower heat but structurally necessary: an age-appropriate peer, heir, duke, official, knight, or noble becomes an active legal/social partner whose recognition changes the heroine's risk and available choices.
- The payoff repairs legal status and secures a freely chosen partnership. Run the romance-removal test before planning.

## Six-Episode Season

1. Family/palace wound: heroine is cast aside, overworked, blamed, divorced, or denied inheritance.
2. Record seed: she finds or preserves a document, ledger, marriage contradiction, or household-account discrepancy.
3. New household/palace role: she enters a place where her overlooked competence matters.
4. Domestic pressure: old family, former spouse, palace faction, or inheritance rival attacks her standing and romantic alliance.
5. Legal/social inquiry: records, staff, the love interest's costly action, or officials reveal the truth.
6. Chosen partnership: legal status changes and the romance becomes publicly secure on the heroine's terms.

## Required Payoffs

- Domestic wound.
- Record or legal object.
- Practical competence.
- Family/palace witness plus a consequential love-interest choice.
- Status correction.
- Chosen-partnership warmth.

## Avoid

- Treating family abuse as endless suffering with no procedural progress.
- Legal reversals without documents or witnesses.
- Child, parenting, custody, adoption, or guardianship plots entering through an inheritance pretext.
- A legal victory that remains unchanged when the primary romance is removed.
