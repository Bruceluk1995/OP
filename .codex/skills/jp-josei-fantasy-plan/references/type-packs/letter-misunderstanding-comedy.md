# Type Pack: Letter Misunderstanding / Romantic Comedy

Use for 手紙, すれ違い, mistaken engagement, misunderstood spouse, false confession, wrong recipient, epistolary romance, and comedy-forward romance with social stakes.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as structural references: letter object, misunderstanding ladder, social embarrassment, reveal timing, and romantic sincerity beneath comedy. If available, read `对标/letter-misunderstanding-comedy/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The engine starts from a concrete communication error: letter, invitation, contract copy, diary, magic message, name confusion, rumor chain, or proxy confession.
- The misunderstanding must create action, not only confusion.
- Comedy works when every wrong assumption has a social cost or romantic pressure.
- The male lead and heroine should each be reasonable from their own information.
- The reveal should deepen affection or expose another plot, not simply end the story.

## Six-Episode Season

1. Wrong message: a letter or message creates an unexpected engagement, visit, rumor, or promise.
2. Acting on error: heroine and male lead make choices that make sense under the misunderstanding.
3. Social complication: salon, family, court, or church witnesses the false premise.
4. Almost reveal: one truth appears, but a deeper assumption remains wrong.
5. Sincere choice: one side protects the other even before full clarity.
6. Reveal and re-choice: the error is exposed, but the couple chooses the real relationship.

## Required Payoffs

- Physical or magical message object.
- Wrong assumption.
- Social witness.
- Action caused by the misunderstanding.
- Sincere protection before reveal.
- Re-choice after reveal.

## Avoid

- Confusion that could be solved by one obvious sentence with no reason for silence.
- Comedy that humiliates the heroine without payoff.
- Reveals that erase all stakes.
