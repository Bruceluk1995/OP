# Type Pack: Academy / Game-World / Mob Knowledge

Use for game-world reincarnation, mob character, villain route, academy exams, class/job traps, death flags, hidden bosses, original story knowledge, or "原作知識で無双".

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as structural references: known route, bad ending, hidden class, exam loophole, boss weakness. If available, read `对标/academy-game-knowledge/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The protagonist knows a system, route, or future event that locals do not.
- The payoff comes from changing an expected bad result while looking ordinary.
- Tension comes from incomplete canon, route deviation, suspicion, limited resources, and saving people without exposing the source of knowledge.

## Six-Episode Season

1. Route recognition: protagonist identifies the world/academy/game and one near-term death flag.
2. First loophole: uses small knowledge to pass exam, avoid trap, or save a minor character.
3. Hidden mechanic: unlocks class/job/item route that the original protagonist missed.
4. Route deviation: saved person or changed event creates a new complication.
5. Hidden boss or rival: knowledge gives an edge but not full certainty.
6. Bad-ending break: protagonist prevents a major failure; next season hook is a route no longer matching memory.

## Required Payoffs

- Known route or future flag.
- Local misunderstanding.
- Knowledge applied through action.
- Changed outcome.
- New uncertainty caused by deviation.

## Avoid

- Dumping game lore in exposition.
- Perfect foreknowledge with no cost.
- Copying an actual game's route or characters.
