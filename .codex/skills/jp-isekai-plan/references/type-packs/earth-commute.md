# Type Pack: Earth Commute / Modern Supply

Use for 日本往返, modern goods, Earth supply, one-day return, supermarket cheat, medicine, food, tools, batteries, online knowledge, or safe retreat.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as functional references: access limit, supply chain, secrecy, culture mismatch, rescue, inflation of value. If available, read `对标/earth-commute/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The cheat is not raw combat; it is asymmetric access.
- Modern goods must create problems as well as solutions: suspicion, pricing, scarcity, transport limit, contamination, dependency, or identity risk.
- The protagonist's advantage should be concrete: medicine, salt, sugar, tools, batteries, maps, books, food preservation, hygiene, simple machines.

## Six-Episode Season

1. First return: protagonist confirms the rule and saves themselves with one cheap modern item.
2. Value proof: a local problem is solved by a mundane item, creating a witness and demand.
3. Secrecy cost: someone notices supply inconsistency; protagonist must build a cover story.
4. Supply chain: limit forces prioritization; wrong item or overuse causes a new risk.
5. Rescue or zamaa: modern knowledge/item exposes fraud, saves a patient, or beats a stronger faction's assumption.
6. Trade route myth: protagonist gains a protected base or patron; next hook is a faction trying to control the source.

## Required Payoffs

- Clear access rule and limit.
- Specific modern item or knowledge.
- Local value proof.
- Secrecy or supply consequence.
- Cross-world hook.

## Avoid

- Infinite shopping with no limit.
- Modern tech solving everything without maintenance.
- Turning the story into a catalog of products.
