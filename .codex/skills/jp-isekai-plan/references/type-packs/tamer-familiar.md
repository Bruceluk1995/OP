# Type Pack: Tamer / Familiar / Monster Party

Use for 従魔, 使い魔, テイマー, monster companion, slime/wolf/dragon egg, pet growth, monster party, evolution, naming, feeding, or skill sharing.

## Benchmark Protocol

Use hot works only as functional references: weak monster growth, naming/evolution, public reaction, companion behavior, monster ecology. If available, read `对标/tamer-familiar/{work}/拆文报告.md`.

## Core Engine

- The companion starts underestimated, strange, injured, low-rank, or socially unacceptable.
- Growth must be visible through behavior, skill, form, bond, evolution, or party role.
- The protagonist's skill is care plus command: food, naming, training, trust, habitat, and tactical use.

## Six-Episode Season

1. First bond: protagonist saves or tames a low-status monster; it solves one small danger.
2. Behavior problem: companion causes trouble; protagonist learns its rule.
3. First evolution/use: food, name, or battle trigger unlocks a visible skill.
4. Public reaction: guild/village/party sees the companion's value and danger.
5. Monster ecology: a stronger monster or clan reacts to the companion.
6. Team identity: protagonist and companion clear a threat together; next hook is evolution, species secret, or monster faction.

## Required Payoffs

- Monster personality.
- Care/training action.
- Tactical companion use.
- Evolution or new behavior.
- Public reaction.

## Avoid

- Companion as decoration only.
- Instant obedience without bond cost.
- Copying mascot designs or named monster species from known works.
