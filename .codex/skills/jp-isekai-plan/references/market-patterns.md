# Market Patterns

Use when designing a Japanese male-audience isekai plan.

These are market-level signals only. For actual planning, follow `dynamic-benchmarking.md`: project拆文 or fresh website benchmark first, project subtype notes second, bundled `references/type-packs/{lane}.md` only as fallback scaffolding.

## Reliable Engines

- Weak start + unusual cheat: the cheat looks useless, then proves valuable through optimization.
- OP dominance / ruler fantasy: protagonist is already overwhelming; the hook is enemy misread, hidden identity, subordinate awe, strategic restraint, public myth growth, and the consequences of one controlled display of power.
- Battle leveling / monster grind: weak skill gets one combat use, protagonist kills a monster above his assumed rank, earns a drop, unlocks a skill variant, and attracts a stronger threat.
- Dungeon boss/drop loop: new floor rule -> monster weakness -> skill combo -> visible drop/rank result -> boss gate or rare quest.
- Exile support reversal: rear guard, healer, appraiser, porter, production worker, or E-rank skill is removed from the party; the old party collapses because logistics, buffs, debuffs, scouting, repair, storage, or boss mechanics were invisible.
- Isekai return / Earth commute: the apparent weak skill is access to Japan, modern goods, medicine, food, batteries, internet-like knowledge, or safe retreat; secrecy and supply become the plot engine.
- Academy / mob / game knowledge: protagonist knows a game route, hidden class, death flag, exam rule, heroine/villain route, or boss weakness; the payoff is changing an expected bad event.
- Tamer/familiar escalation: a low-status monster, pet, slime, wolf, bird, dragon egg, or familiar becomes stronger through care, naming, food, skill sharing, or evolution.
- Dungeon master / territory engine: traps, floors, resources, monster placement, town economy, guild access, and defense against rival powers create爽点.
- Secret society / subordinate snowball: protagonist's accidental command creates a powerful group; subordinates misread vague orders brilliantly, enemies inflate the myth, and the protagonist manages the consequences.
- Production/crafting: repair, appraisal, cooking, alchemy, taming, farming, item slots, enchantment, or logistics.
- Public proof: a skeptical guild worker, noble, senior adventurer, or bully watches the protagonist solve a visible problem.
- Zamaa light: the opponent overreaches, the protagonist wins cleanly, witnesses react, reward is practical.
- Slow life resistance: protagonist wants a quiet life, but the cheat's usefulness attracts requests.
- Party/familiar loop: companion growth, monster taming, pet/familiar strength, comic reactions.
- Status/skill loop: new skill discovered, tested, named, limited, then combined.

## Subtype Menu For New Users

When guiding a new male-audience user, show a wider menu than slow life:

- 开局无敌/龙傲天: OP dominance, ruler, demon lord, hidden boss, secret organization.
- 打怪升级: dungeon, monster grind, drops, ranks, boss gates, skill variants.
- 追放ざまぁ: support class or weak-looking skill removed from party, old party collapses.
- 主人公最強/いずれ最強: academy, training, hidden talent, evaluation failure, tournament/exam.
- 游戏/原作知识: mob reincarnation, death-game, route knowledge, hidden boss.
- 日本往返/现代物资: Earth commute, supply chain, food, medicine, rescue, secrecy.
- 従魔/テイマー: monster companion growth, evolution, party reactions.
- ダンジョンマスター/领地经营: base, traps, subordinates, resources, defense.
- 生产/商会/慢生活: keep as optional hybrid, not the default爽文 path.

## Episode 1 Must Usually Do

1. Put the protagonist under concrete pressure.
2. Show why the world is unfair or expensive.
3. Reveal the cheat through a small object or low-stakes experiment.
4. Let the cheat solve a practical problem.
5. Give the protagonist a modest reward that changes his next choice.
6. End with a larger problem or opportunity.

## Avoid

- Starting with long cosmology.
- Translating Chinese power systems one-to-one.
- Abstract "cultivation" progress without visible RPG/social proof.
- Overpowered victory before the reader understands the rule.
- OP protagonist with no restraint, information problem, subordinate/faction pressure, reputation consequence, or larger-stage hook; instant wins alone go flat.
- Defaulting every male-audience premise to shopkeeping, herbs, or quiet base-building when the user asked for爽文, battle, dungeon, monster, level-up, or zamaa.
- Copying famous OP/ruler works directly. Extract the function; replace characters, factions, setting, and event sequence.
