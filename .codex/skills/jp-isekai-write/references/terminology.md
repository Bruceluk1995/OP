# Terminology and Leakage Scan

Before finalizing a Japanese isekai episode adapted from Chinese material, scan for:

`方玄|白石坊|灵|靈|妖|修炼|修煉|宗门|宗門|世家|筑基|金丹|元婴|玄|仙|龟|龜|灵根|灵气|妖兽|妖獸|灵兽|靈獸|坊市|洞府|丹药|丹藥|法宝|法寶|灵石|靈石|飞剑|飛劍|道侣|道侶`

Replace with Japanese RPG/fantasy equivalents:

- `魔力`, `魔素`, `スキル`, `ステータス`
- `冒険者ギルド`, `商会`, `貴族家`, `魔法学院`
- `魔物`, `使い魔`, `従魔`, `家畜`
- `銅貨`, `銀貨`, `金貨`, `魔石`
- `ポーション`, `魔道具`, `アーティファクト`

If the user specifically asks for "no Chinese elements", also avoid Chinese-flavored names, sect ranks, Daoist imagery, flying swords, cultivation stages, and beast names that feel like xianxia taxonomy.
