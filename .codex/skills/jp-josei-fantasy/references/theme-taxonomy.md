# 女性向け異世界恋愛 Theme Taxonomy

Use this as a seed taxonomy for Japanese female-reader fantasy romance. If the user asks for current ranking, latest hot tags, or platform-specific market proof, browse live sources before making current claims.

## Top Theme Seeds

1. `異世界転生 / 異世界転移` - Heroine starts over with memory or displacement, using foreknowledge or modern sensibility to survive and love again.
2. `悪役令嬢` - Heroine becomes the condemned noble villainess and tries to avoid ruin while changing the romance route.
3. `婚約破棄` - Public annulment humiliates the heroine, then opens a better path to status, love, and justice.
4. `ざまぁ` - Readers get catharsis when abusers, cheaters, false saints, or greedy relatives face earned consequences.
5. `溺愛` - A powerful male lead loves, protects, and recognizes the heroine with unusually deep devotion.
6. `乙女ゲーム世界` - Heroine knows the game route and works around flags, capture targets, and bad endings.
7. `実は有能な令嬢 / 隠された才能` - The underestimated heroine proves her ability through magic, management, healing, etiquette, or knowledge.
8. `辺境伯 / 公爵 / 侯爵令嬢` - High noble status, frontier authority, and marriage politics create prestige and pressure.
9. `家族冷遇 -> 高位男性救済` - A neglected daughter is recognized by a prince, duke, or frontier lord and regains dignity.
10. `スローライフ` - After rejection or exile, the heroine builds a peaceful life that attracts love and new conflict.
11. `契約婚 / 仮初めの婚約` - A fake arrangement becomes emotionally real through care, trust, and shared danger.
12. `追放 / 辺境追放` - Banishment becomes liberation, competence proof, and a new home.
13. `聖女` - Sacred power, replacement saints, church politics, healing, and false accusation drive the plot.
14. `復讐もの` - The heroine systematically exposes or defeats those who harmed her.
15. `呪われた王子 / 皇帝 / 王弟` - A cursed or feared royal man is healed or understood by the heroine.
16. `領地経営 / 内政` - The heroine improves territory, trade, agriculture, or welfare and gains public trust.
17. `現代知識活用` - Food, hygiene, accounting, cosmetics, medicine, or management become fantasy advantages.
18. `魔女 / 魔導師` - Female magic expertise enables independence, danger, and romance.
19. `後宮 / 宮廷ロマンス` - Palace factions, succession, etiquette, and jealousy shape romance and survival.
20. `義妹 / 異母妹に奪われる` - A stepsister or half-sister steals fiance, credit, family love, or saint status.
21. `ハッピーエンド` - The ending promise is love, safety, social restoration, and future belonging.
22. `騎士 / 黒騎士` - A loyal or feared knight protects the heroine while learning tenderness.
23. `魔王 / 黒魔導師溺愛` - A dark powerful male lead becomes devoted to one heroine.
24. `逆ハーレム` - Multiple excellent men are drawn to the heroine; use carefully if the user wants a single endgame.
25. `実は王族 / 隠し身分` - Hidden royal or noble identity creates a late reversal.
26. `タイムリープ / ループ` - The heroine repeats time to avoid tragedy and choose love.
27. `コメディ / 勘違い` - Misunderstanding, etiquette comedy, and light banter soften revenge or romance.
28. `和風 / 料理もの` - Japanese aesthetics or food knowledge create comfort, business, and intimacy.
29. `百合要素` - Female bonds or romance elements are foregrounded when requested.
30. `チート能力 / 最強令嬢` - The heroine has overwhelming ability, but the emotional promise still matters more than power scaling.
