## Control Options

- `0`：由 Codex 根据推文传播性自动挑选并说明理由。
- `37`：混合两个或三个题材，生成复合型设定。
- `38`：用户输入自己的题材或关键词。
- `39`：换一批更冷门、更怪或更新鲜的题材。

## Interaction Rules

- Say `可多选`, with examples such as `2+9`, `14+16`, or `22+30`.
- Do not treat a selected broad topic as permission to decide every plot detail. After selection, propose 3 compact premise variants when meaningful.
- If the user selects more than three topics, warn that the premise may become crowded and offer one primary engine plus secondary flavors.
- For multiple presentation-mode selections and multiple topics, avoid a combinatorial explosion: confirm which dimensions should share the same premise before drafting full-length versions.
- If the user dislikes the menu, accept free-form keywords immediately; never force a numbered choice.
