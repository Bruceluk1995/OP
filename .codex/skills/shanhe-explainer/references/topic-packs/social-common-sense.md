# Topic Pack: Social Common Sense

Use for everyday incentives, work, class, public order, policing, homelessness, addiction, status, leadership, education, family/social norms, and "why do people behave like this" questions.

## Core Engine

- Start from a rude but recognizable observation.
- Replace moral scolding with incentives, risk, selection, social cost, and survival strategy.
- Show how ordinary people adapt to a system before judging them.
- End with a cold but useful sentence: the behavior is not an exception; it is what the environment selects.

## Best Openings

- Workplace meeting, school rule, station platform, hospital queue, bank counter, police encounter, rental contract, family dinner.
- A social phrase that viewers already know, then a reversal.
- One person's "bad attitude" that turns out to be a rational adaptation.

## Required Payoffs

- Human incentive.
- Social risk.
- Institution or environment.
- Non-preachy final judgment.

## Avoid

- Punching down at vulnerable groups.
- Treating addiction, poverty, illness, disability, migration, or crime as cheap jokes.
- Culture-war certainty without mechanism.
