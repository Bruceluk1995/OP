# Audience Pack: Japan

Use for Japanese-language scripts and Japan-facing economics/finance angles.

## Native Anxiety Points

- Yen weakness and import prices.
- Wages, low growth, and household purchasing power.
- Aging, pensions, healthcare, and local decline.
- Low-rate legacy, mortgages, savings, and risk products.
- Company hierarchy, long work hours, side jobs, and skill inflation.
- Convenience, service quality, and who pays for that labor.

## Opening Objects

- salary notification;
- convenience-store price tag;
- electricity bill;
- yen exchange-rate headline;
- pension statement;
- job-transfer notice;
- station ad for investment products;
- local shopping street with closed shutters.

## Style

- Calm `です/ます` narration.
- Dry irony, not loud accusation.
- Explain institutional tradeoffs before moral judgment.
- Use Japan's own systems and anxieties, not imported Chinese internet shortcuts.

## Avoid

- "Japanese people are just..." explanations.
- China-centered examples unless the topic requires comparison.
- Personal investment advice.
