# Audience Pack: Europe

Use for European, EU, UK, or country-specific economics/finance explainers.

## Native Anxiety Points

- Energy bills, inflation, ECB or national rate policy.
- Housing shortage, rent pressure, youth unemployment.
- Welfare-state funding, taxes, pensions, aging.
- EU rules versus national politics.
- Industrial policy, migration, regional inequality.

## Opening Objects

- energy bill;
- rent renewal;
- train ticket;
- tax notice;
- supermarket price label;
- pension debate;
- EU regulation headline.

## Style

- Compare institutions carefully. Europe is not one market in everyday life.
- If country-specific, name the country and do not generalize across Europe casually.
- Use welfare-state tradeoffs and energy dependence when relevant.

## Avoid

- Treating the EU as a single country.
- US-only investment assumptions.
- China-centered examples unless explicitly comparative.
