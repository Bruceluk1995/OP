# Topic Pack: Household Money

Use for personal finance education, household budget pressure, consumption traps, education costs, career choice, debt, rent, mortgage burden, retirement anxiety, and sunk-cost decisions.

## Core Engine

- Start from a small wallet event: payday, rent payment, credit-card bill, student loan notice, subscription renewal, childcare bill, parent-care cost.
- Avoid scolding people for poor discipline. Ask what fixed costs, wage structure, debt terms, or social expectations make the choice hard.
- Translate concepts into ordinary rules: cash flow, opportunity cost, sunk cost, compound interest, present bias, insurance, emergency fund, liquidity.

## Required Payoffs

- One concrete household scene.
- One mechanism beyond "people spend too much."
- One risk-transfer or cost-shifting point.
- One practical educational rule without personalized advice.

## Title Shapes

- `为什么你明明没乱花钱，却越来越存不下钱？`
- `なぜ普通の人ほど、お金が残りにくいのか`
- `Why Your Budget Breaks Even When You Did Nothing Crazy`
- `沉没成本为什么最容易困住普通人？`

## Avoid

- Personalized budgeting advice.
- Moralizing about laziness or vanity.
- One-size-fits-all savings ratios.
