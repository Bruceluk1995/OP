# Topic Pack: Platform Labor Capital

Use for AI economy, platform work, creator economy, monopoly, pricing power, subscriptions, traffic allocation, labor bargaining, productivity, automation, and capital-labor distribution.

## Core Engine

- Start with an efficiency promise: AI saves time, apps bring customers, platforms give freedom.
- Ask who owns the tool, traffic, data, pricing rule, and customer relationship.
- Show why productivity gains do not automatically become leisure or higher wages.

## Required Payoffs

- One worker/creator/small-business scene.
- One platform or ownership mechanism.
- One distribution question.
- One rule about bargaining power or control of tools.

## Title Shapes

- `AI帮你省下的时间，为什么不一定属于你？`
- `なぜ効率化しても、仕事は楽にならないのか`
- `Who Owns the Time AI Saves?`
- `平台给你流量，也给你定价`

## Avoid

- Saying "technology is bad."
- Treating AI as magic.
- Ignoring consumers, advertisers, platform rules, and competition.
