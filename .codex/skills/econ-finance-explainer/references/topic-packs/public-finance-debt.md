# Topic Pack: Public Finance And Debt

Use for government debt, local finance, infrastructure, taxes, pensions, healthcare finance, municipal bonds, public services, subsidies, and fiscal tradeoffs.

## Core Engine

- Start with a visible public service or project: road, station, school, hospital, pension office, tax notice.
- Ask what the government is trying to buy: growth, stability, votes, time, employment, or service continuity.
- Trace the bill: current taxpayers, future taxpayers, users, pension contributors, bondholders, or inflation.

## Required Payoffs

- One public object.
- One budget constraint.
- One intergenerational or regional tradeoff.
- One reason the obvious fix is politically or economically hard.

## Title Shapes

- `为什么地方越缺钱，越要搞建设？`
- `誰が公共サービスの請求書を払っているのか`
- `Why Public Debt Is Often a Time Machine`
- `养老金不是一个账户问题，而是人口结构问题`

## Avoid

- Treating all public spending as waste.
- Treating all debt as evil.
- Ignoring demographics and tax base.
