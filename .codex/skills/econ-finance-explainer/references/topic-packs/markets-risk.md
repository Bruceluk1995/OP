# Topic Pack: Markets And Risk

Use for stocks, IPOs, funds, ETFs, bonds, real estate finance, securitization, leverage, margin, derivatives, liquidity, bank runs, defaults, and bubbles.

## Core Engine

- Follow the contract, not the slogan.
- Ask who gets fees, who owns upside, who holds downside, who needs liquidity, and who can exit first.
- Use simple analogies: ticket queues, layered cakes, casino tables, fire exits, insurance pools, buckets with holes.

## Required Payoffs

- One market promise.
- One contract or balance-sheet mechanism.
- One risk-transfer point.
- One "retail viewer should understand this before believing the ad" rule.

## Title Shapes

- `等上市再买，为什么常常已经晚了？`
- `なぜ「安全そうな利回り」ほど、先に契約を読むべきなのか`
- `This Is Not Free Yield. It Is Someone Else's Risk.`
- `金融包装如何把坏账变成好故事？`

## Avoid

- Trade calls.
- Predicting short-term asset prices.
- Recommending specific securities.
- Explaining leverage without explaining liquidation or liquidity.
