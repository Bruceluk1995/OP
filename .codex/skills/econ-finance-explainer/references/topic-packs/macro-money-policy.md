# Topic Pack: Macro Money Policy

Use for inflation, deflation, interest rates, exchange rates, central banks, recession, wage-price dynamics, money supply, fiscal-monetary conflict, and macro cycles.

## Core Engine

- Start from a daily price or wage feeling, then move upward to central banks, supply, currency, and expectations.
- Explain tradeoffs. Macro policy rarely gives a free lunch.
- Show how one policy solves one pain while creating another.

## Required Payoffs

- One everyday price or income anchor.
- One verified macro data point when current claims matter.
- One policy tradeoff.
- One distribution question: who benefits first, who pays later.

## Title Shapes

- `为什么降息不一定让普通人更轻松？`
- `円安はなぜ、家計の問題になるのか`
- `Why Inflation Feels Like a Pay Cut`
- `When Cheap Money Becomes Expensive`

## Avoid

- Claiming central banks can simply print prosperity.
- Treating inflation, rates, or exchange rates as moral failure.
- Using old data without dates.
