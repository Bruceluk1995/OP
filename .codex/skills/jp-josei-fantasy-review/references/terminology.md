# Terminology And Localization

Use this list when reviewing for Chinese leakage.

## Red Flags

- `修仙`, `修炼`, `境界`, `筑基`, `金丹`, `元婴`, `飞升`.
- `灵气`, `灵根`, `灵兽`, `仙门`, `宗门`, `门派`, `师尊`.
- `丹药`, `符箓`, `法宝`, `阵法` when used in cultivation flavor.
- Chinese-style `嫡女`, `庶女`, `皇子夺嫡`, `后宫争宠` unless the user requested Chinese court fantasy.
- Literal Chinese court-drama surfaces such as `皇帝`, `皇上`, `圣旨`, `贵妃`, `妃嫔`, `后宫`, `冷宫`, `宫女`, `太监`, `嬷嬷`, `赐婚`, `抄家`, `灭族`, `朝堂` when the target is Japanese female fantasy.
- RPG `level/status/skill tree` dominance when the story promise is romance and social reversal.

## Safer JP Female-Fantasy Replacements

- Magic: `魔力`, `加護`, `祝福`, `聖属性`, `魔導`, `精霊`.
- Institutions: `神殿`, `魔法学院`, `騎士団`, `王宮`, `貴族院`, `商会`.
- Family/social: `令嬢`, `異母妹`, `後妻`, `王妃教育`, `社交界`, `領地`.
- Romance/legal: `婚約破棄`, `契約結婚`, `仮初めの婚約`, `政略結婚`, `白い結婚`.
- Roles: `聖女`, `魔女`, `治癒師`, `騎士団長`, `辺境伯`, `公爵`, `王太子`.

## Court-Drama Adaptation Check

Do not judge only vocabulary. Check whether the Chinese court-drama function was localized:

- emperor order -> `王命`, `勅命`, `王太子の婚約`, or `貴族院/神殿の公的判断`.
- harem ranking -> `王妃`, `側妃`, `婚約者`, `愛妾`, `宮廷派閥`, or remove the harem structure entirely.
- legitimate/concubine-born conflict -> `正妻の娘`, `後妻の娘`, `異母妹`, `相続順位`, `家督争い`.
- cold palace/punishment -> `離宮に幽閉`, `修道院送り`, `塔への幽閉`, `王都追放`, `爵位剥奪`.
- palace servants/eunuch logic -> `侍女`, `女官`, `侍従`, `執事`, `神官`.

If the draft still feels like a Chinese palace drama with Japanese words pasted on top, mark it as a localization failure.
