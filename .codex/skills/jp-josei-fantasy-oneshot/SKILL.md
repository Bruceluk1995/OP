Infer from the prompt whenever possible:

Do not present a menu when these are clear. Ask only for an essential missing choice or make a safe stated assumption.

## Defaults

- Audience-facing body: natural Japanese; meta notes: Chinese unless requested otherwise.
- Default complete one-shot budget: approximately 12,000 Japanese characters in either presentation mode.
- Treat 12,000 as a target, not a minimum gate or permission to pad.
- Complete the heroine's wound/choice, relationship recognition, social consequence, and satisfying future in this file.

## Execution

Run the studio fast path once: Producer -> Story Core -> Change Structure -> Japanese Scene Writer -> Blind Editor. Restart from the earliest failed stage. First person must use the studio knowledge/voice contract.

Browse only for current market, ranking, trend, news, live-source requests, or an explicitly selected Chinese women-oriented novel-site inspiration pass. Chinese novel-site popularity is cross-market inspiration only; require a current Japanese cross-check before calling the premise Japanese-market validated. Opening cards, surface lint, package files, cover prompts, character prompts, and ledgers are optional.

For project-bound work, save the Japanese body and one compact decision card. Create a full package only when requested.

## Female-Romance Closure

- The heroine makes at least one costly choice that changes evidence, relationship, location, or status.
- Romance grows through specific recognition, respect, shared action, and mutual choice.
- After decisive evidence, the story changes problem instead of staging more public proof.
- Zamaa/social consequence follows from the antagonist's action and the heroine's agency.
- The ending makes dignity, relationship, or secure future visible; no previous/next-episode bridge.

## Hard Rules

- Do not make the heroine wait for the male lead, a document, or an institution to solve the story.
- Do not repeat accusation -> evidence -> crowd reaction in three venues.
- Do not translate Chinese harem,嫡庶, cold-palace,赐婚, or court systems literally.
- Do not equate 溺愛 with control or make the heroine a reward object.
- Do not call length, opening-card, or surface-lint results quality approval.
- Do not present Chinese women-oriented novel-site popularity as Japanese-market validation or copy its titles, systems, characters, scene order, or named plot.
