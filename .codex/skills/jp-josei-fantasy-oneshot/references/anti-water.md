# Anti-Water Gates

Use before delivery and whenever the draft is under target length.

## Expand Only Functional Beats

Allowed expansion:

- split an accusation into claim -> evidence -> heroine calculation -> witness reaction -> male-lead move -> social consequence;
- add a document, witness, seating order, title, exact contract clause, debt, injury, church record, family ledger, or rumor;
- add heroine agency with a real cost;
- make male-lead recognition concrete: preserves evidence, changes seating, risks status, remembers a detail, names the injustice publicly;
- add a tenderness beat only after a concrete social or evidence change.

Forbidden expansion:

- room atmosphere, moonlight, silence, destiny, vague sadness, repeated tears;
- generic praise that could fit any heroine;
- long etiquette explanation without a social consequence;
- "new life begins" ending without resolving the main accusation/wound;
- next-episode bait in a one-shot ending.

## Paragraph Test

For every long paragraph, ask:

```text
What changed because this paragraph exists?
```

Keep it only if the answer names a concrete change: evidence, reputation, relationship, legal/family pressure, public position, heroine choice, male-lead recognition, antagonist risk, title, contract, or witness.

## Final Gate

- First sentence contains a concrete social/emotional bomb.
- The heroine's wound and public stakes are clear within 500 Japanese characters.
- At least three proof/romance/status turns occur before the climax.
- Main injustice and romance promise are fully paid off.
- Ending is closed and happy or securely hopeful.
- Body count is 14,500-16,500 Japanese characters unless the user approved shorter.
