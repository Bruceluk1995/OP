# Josei Fantasy One-Shot Blueprint

Use before drafting every standalone female-audience fantasy romance short.

Plan for colloquial, direct prose. Every beat should move through action, dialogue, choice, evidence, romance movement, or social consequence; do not reserve space for long scenery, atmosphere, beauty, sadness, or psychology passages.

## One-Shot Rhythm

For 15k one-shots, use 12-18 meaningful beats. Every 2,000-3,000 characters should deliver at least one visible proof, romantic recognition, heroine choice, social cost, public reversal, or tenderness payoff.

Recommended distribution:

- 0-1,500 chars: opening social/emotional bomb, heroine wound, public stakes.
- 1,500-4,000 chars: first heroine choice and first evidence or romance pressure.
- 4,000-7,000 chars: antagonist escalation, family/court/church/contract pressure.
- 7,000-10,000 chars: midpoint recognition, male lead action, heroine agency cost.
- 10,000-13,500 chars: public proof, zamaa, legal/social consequence.
- 13,500-16,500 chars: romance confirmation, dignity recovery, closed happy ending.

## Subtype Payoff Floors

- 婚約破棄ざまぁ: accusation, evidence object, witness, public venue, legal/social consequence, better-match recognition.
- 悪役令嬢: route flag, changed choice, public misread, fate pressure, new dignity.
- 聖女/church: false evaluation, ritual/healing rule, church record, witness, cost, correction.
- 契約結婚: contract clause, forced proximity, household proof, public spouse recognition, real choice.
- 辺境伯/溺愛: old rejection, new home problem, protective respect, public home choice.
- 職人令嬢: named product/service, customer proof, money/reputation/access change, romance through respect for work.
- 相続家族: will, ledger, title, child/stepfamily bond, legal correction.
- ループ: known flag, changed choice, unexpected ripple, hidden-cause proof.
- 異類婚姻: cultural rule, nonhuman tenderness, ritual/public choice, human-side threat resolved.
- 強いヒロイン: visible capability, status pressure, risk, male-lead respect for competence.
- 手紙すれ違い: message object, wrong assumption, witness, action caused by misunderstanding, sincere re-choice.

## Cultural Localization Floor

If the source premise comes from Chinese 古言、宫斗、朝堂、玄幻、穿书, identify the original function before writing and rebuild it for Japanese female-audience fantasy romance.

- 皇帝/圣旨/赐婚 -> 王、王命、王太子、王宮、貴族院、婚約命令.
- 后妃/贵妃/后宫争宠 -> 王妃、側妃、愛妾、婚約者、社交界派閥; avoid harem-ranking logic unless the setting truly needs it.
- 嫡女/庶女/嫡庶 -> 公爵家の長女、正妻の娘、後妻の娘、異母妹、相続順位.
- 冷宫/打入冷宫 -> 離宮に幽閉、修道院送り、塔への幽閉、王都追放.
- 太监/宫女/嬷嬷 -> 侍従、侍女、女官、執事、老侍女.
- 抄家/灭族/株连 -> 爵位剥奪、領地没収、一族追放、処刑判決, with proportional romance-fantasy consequences.
- 宫斗/朝堂权谋 -> 社交界の失脚、宮廷派閥争い、神殿の告発、貴族院の審問、婚約破棄ざまぁ.

## Ending Rule

Close the central promise:

- heroine's dignity restored;
- public accusation corrected or antagonist exposed;
- romance recognition made concrete;
- heroine makes a real choice;
- happy ending or hopeful secured future is visible.

Do not end with an unresolved witness, accusation, contract clause, or romantic question. If one appears at the end, it must symbolize the resolved future, not tease the next episode.
